Upwyze Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 Oct 2025

Who we are

Upwyze (“Upwyze”, “we”, “us”, “our”) provides a platform for students and tutors to discover each other, chat, book online or in-person sessions, join workshops, and handle payments and tutor payouts. Our website is https://www.upwyze.net/home.

Scope

This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information when you use our mobile apps, website (https://www.upwyze.net/home), and related services (the “Services”).

Information we collect

Account & profile

• Name, email, phone number, password hash
• Role (student or tutor)
• Profile details tutors or students add (bio, subjects, city, avatar)
• Referral codes you enter or share

Bookings, workshops & messages

• Booking/session and workshop details
• In-app messages/chats and reviews/ratings

Payments & wallet

• Top-ups, purchases, refunds, and withdrawal/payout records
• Tutor payout details you provide (for example, account or Instapay identifier and account name)

We do not store full card numbers on our servers. Payments and payouts are processed by third-party providers.

Device & usage

• App identifiers (for example, internal user ID), device push token (for notifications)
• Logs and usage information needed to operate, secure, and improve the Services

Website cookies

• Cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in and provide core website features

Location

Upwyze may ask you to add a city or area for in-person availability. We do not automatically collect precise GPS location.

How we use information

• Provide core features (profiles, chat, booking, workshops, wallet, payouts)
• Send transactional messages and notifications (for example, booking changes and status updates)
• Customer support and service improvement
• Safety, fraud prevention, and enforcement of our Terms
• Legal and financial compliance (for example, transaction records)

Sharing

We share information only as needed to run the Services:

• Service providers (hosting, email or SMS, analytics, payment and payout processors)
• Legal and safety purposes (to comply with law, enforce agreements, or protect users and rights)
• Business transfers (for example, merger or acquisition)

We do not sell personal data and do not use third-party advertising SDKs.

Payments & payouts

Customer payments and tutor payouts are handled by third-party processors. Those providers process your payment data under their own policies. Upwyze stores limited records such as amounts, status, and timestamps for accounting and compliance.

Data retention

We keep personal information while your account is active and as needed to provide the Services and meet legal, tax, and accounting requirements. When you request deletion, we delete or anonymize data not required to be kept.

Your choices & rights

• View, update, or delete profile information in the app
• Turn off push notifications in your device settings
• Request a copy or deletion of your data (subject to legal exceptions) by emailing info@upwyze.net

Children

Upwyze is intended for users aged 13+ (or the minimum age in your country). If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact info@upwyze.net and we will delete it.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards such as encryption in transit, access controls, and least-privilege practices. No method is 100% secure, but we work continuously to protect your information.

International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to protect your information in accordance with this policy and applicable law.

Third-party links

Our Services may link to third-party websites. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Account deletion

Instructions for deleting your Upwyze account are available here: Account Deletion.

This page explains how to request deletion and what data may be retained for legal, tax, accounting, fraud-prevention, or safety reasons.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new version here and update the “Last updated” date.
