Account Deletion – Upwyze

You can delete your Upwyze account directly from the app:

  1. Open Upwyze
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm

If you cannot access the app, email us from your registered email:

Support: support@upwyze.net

What will be deleted: account profile, bookings, chat history, and wallet history associated with the account.

Processing time: typically within 7 business days.
